# 🧠 Brand Interpretation (Helpful Insight)

* The **“elves” concept** = doing work for you (efficient helpers)
* The **paper stack** = productivity / task handling
* The **house** = real estate or home-related services
* The **color scheme** = warm (trust + energy) + stable (professionalism)
