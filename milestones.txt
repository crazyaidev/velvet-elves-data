KEY DEPENDENCIES & RISKS
================================================================================

1. Task Template Source Data:
   REWORKING_TASK_DB.csv (or equivalent final mapping source) must be locked by
   Week 2 for task-template import and dependency validation.

2. Approved MVP Design Stability:
   The approved HTML references for Active Transactions, Solo Agent, Team
   Leader, Attorney, and FSBO should remain stable by Week 7/Week 17. Late
   layout changes to the shared shell or dashboard modules may cause rework.

3. Third-Party Credentials:
   OpenAI, Supabase, Gmail, Outlook, Apple iCloud, DocuSign/HelloSign, and
   Stripe credentials/sandboxes must be available by the milestone weeks where
   they are first needed.

4. Legal/Operations Policy Decisions:
   State-rule logic, attorney release/disbursement policies, customer-safe
   document visibility rules, and share-link wording need confirmation early
   enough to avoid rework in Weeks 5-17.

5. Integration Access:
   MLS, CRM, title-company, MLO, and calendar-provider access is required by
   Week 20 to validate hook implementations and documentation.

6. Scope Control:
   Advertising, mobile, IMAP/SMTP fallback, predictive analytics,
   role-specific Active Transactions pages, AI Coach, and concierge upsells are
   explicitly non-MVP build items. Expanding them risks timeline and budget
   overrun.

7. Data Migration Responsibility:
   MVP only migrates task-template data from the legacy platform. Brokers still
   need guidance/tools for re-entering or importing transactions/contacts and
   for preserving historical data offline.
