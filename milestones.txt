KEY DEPENDENCIES & RISKS
================================================================================

1. Task Spreadsheet Delivery: Client must provide REWORKING TASK DB.xlsx
   by Week 2 for task catalogue import.

2. API Credentials: Gmail, Outlook, DocuSign, Stripe, and OpenAI API
   credentials/accounts must be available by Week 9 (AI), Week 13 (e-sign),
   Week 14 (email), Week 18 (payments).

3. Legacy Codebase Access: Access to current ASP.NET MVC codebase needed
   by Week 1 for review.

4. Client Feedback Loops: Wizard flow (Week 11) requires client review and
   approval within 2-3 business days to avoid timeline slippage.
   NOTE: Agent/Elf dashboard design approved by client (2026-03-13) and
   moved to Week 8. Team Lead toggle and remaining role dashboards still
   need client review during Week 17.

5. Third-Party API Limitations: MLS feed access, e-signature provider
   sandbox availability, and email provider OAuth approval processes
   may introduce delays.

6. Scope Creep: The advertising module, consumer-facing site, and some
   advanced AI features (NLP task creation, predictive analytics) are
   scoped minimally for MVP. Expanding these risks timeline overrun.
